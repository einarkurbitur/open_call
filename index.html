PS Action Club Open Call

This is an open call for all artists and non-artists to create Photoshop Actions. Download and open an action from the gallery down below to see how it works.

Go to instructions to see how it to upload your action file. In the gallery you can see a preview of our favorite actions and download and run them.

How to run

Open the action from your downloads. Photoshop will launch. open the action menu via Window-->Actions

To change the playback speed, press the button in the top right corner of the action window and select "Playback Options...". From there you can choose to either play it step by step or instantaneous.

This way the work will be shown step by step.

Press play and enjoy!
