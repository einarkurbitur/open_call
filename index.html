A small tutorial on Photoshop actions

Open your Photoshop, create a new file and go to Window->Actions Click on the new folder button and give your folder a name. Click on the new action button and give your action a name. To enable tool recording, press the button in the top right corner of the action window and select "Allow Tool Recording"
 To record your action press the circle button at the bottom of the action window.
 To stop recording press the square button at the bottom of the action window. To play the action press the play button at the bottom of the action window. When your action(s) is ready, press the folder with the action(s) in it. Then press the button in the top right corner of the action window and select "Save Actions..." (Beware you can only export sets, not independent actions.) Name your action "title_of_your_work.atn" and email it to our email new@photoshopactions.com project by: Einar Andersen and Florian van Zandwijk
